✅ Safe Foods (Low Histamine + Low IgE)
  • Protein: Freshly cooked chicken, turkey, lamb
  • Grains: Quinoa, brown rice (confirm with allergist)
  • Oils: Extra virgin olive oil, avocado oil
  • Milk alternatives: Hemp milk, rice milk
  • Fruits: Blueberries, cherries, mango, melon (cantaloupe/honeydew), peach, plum, grapes
    Not: citrus, strawberry, pineapple, avocado, banana, apple, pear, dates
  • Vegetables: Carrots, broccoli, cauliflower, zucchini, cucumber, asparagus, beets, cabbage
    Not: spinach, eggplant, tomato, potato, onion, garlic, corn, beans, peas
  • Fresh herbs: Basil, thyme, parsley (not oregano)
❌ High-Histamine — Always Avoid
  • Fermented foods — sauerkraut, vinegar, soy sauce, kefir, kombucha, pickles
  • Cured / smoked meats — bacon, salami, pepperoni, hot dogs
  • Aged cheese (including goat cheese)
  • Soured foods — sour cream, buttermilk, sourdough
  • Dried fruit, most citrus, avocado, eggplant, spinach, tomato
  • Strawberries, pineapple, banana
  • Leftovers — histamine builds as food sits. Always cook fresh!
⚠️ High IgE Grains — Use Caution
